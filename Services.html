Our Services

We offer a wide variety of services that you can take advantage of for both residential and commericial properties.

Installation

Purchased a new home or business? Let us get it fully wired! From minor installations of ceiling fans and lightbulbs to full-property wiring, we've got you covered.

Repairs

Suffering from tricky electricial problems? Let us handle it! Our professionals can safely and quickly diagnose the problem and fully repair it so that you don't have to worry about it.

Upgrades

Are your lights flickering or losing power? Electricity bill higher than your neighbors? Old outdated wiring? You may need an upgrade to new energy efficient lighting. Let us save you money.

Electrical Supplies

We offer a wide variety of supplies in our warehouse ready to ship. Feel free to call us if you're looking for quality supplies for your DIY projects.

Electrical Inspections

Have a concern in your home? Our professional inspectors will come out and inspect your home to ensure that it's up to code and that you are safe. If an inspector finds an issue, you will get an estimate on the spot!

Security Systems

We offer a variety of home and business security solutions. Whether you need motion activated lighting, surveillance cameras or full security systems, we've got you covered.

Baseboard Heating Installation

Need a more affordable option than the usual central heating systems? Baseboard heaters are a great option that ensures you're not paying to heat unnecessary areas.

Ceiling Fan Installation

A ceiling fan is a great, energy-friendly solution for cooling a room. Let our professionals safely install yours for you!

Emergency Visits

Can't wait that long to receive service? That's okay, we offer emergency service with an additional cost. Available 24/7 any day of the year.

And much more! Give us a call for your free estimate on whatever you need done!
